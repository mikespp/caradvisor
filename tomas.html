About

Tomas is a fesh fruits shop owner. He want a car for delivering products. His store is very big and he sales rare expensive fruits. He decided to deliver by his own because he want to deliver fresh and undamaged fruits to his customers. He ship his product all over the country. So he need the pickup truck that has high stability in case that the country road is not good. And the important thing is volume of the rear space because he use it to load his products.

From the graphs above, BT-50 is the best considered from all the option. Another one that have the same dimension and wheel width is Ford Ranger Wildtrak but BT-50 is cheaper. This pickup is used for business so cheaper one is better option.

RESULT

Mazda BT-50 Pro
